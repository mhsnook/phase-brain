Order-parameter-coupled switching network

Three layers — felt, referential, temporal — whose tendency to lock or fragment depends on each other's current state, following Bick's order-parameter-dependent coupling mechanism (2017). A layer is pulled toward synchrony when its "upstream" neighbor is desynchronized, and toward fragmentation when its "downstream" neighbor is synchronized — producing genuine switching rather than a fixed hierarchy of resilience.
